What is the surface area of a cylinder?
The surface area of a cylinder is the sum of the curved surface and the area of two circular bases of the cylinder.
How to calculate surface area of a cylinder?
To calculate surface area of a cylinder you should use the following formula:
A = 2 * π * r * h + 2 * π * r^2
where:
r = radius
h = height
